CST Transfer Dashboard

CST-Transfer Dashboard is a web-based application designed for Academic Advisors, Students, and Faculty Advisors who are all part of the College of Science and Technology. This dashboard allows Academic Advisors to create tickets for transfer students, assign faculty advisors to a department, and edit a student's ticket information. For Students, the dashboard provides an easy and efficient way for them to upload required information for evaluation while also being able to receive feedback and track the ticket status throughout its lifecycle. For Faculty Advisors, it provides a faster and easier way to evaluate transfer students' courses and provide an evaluation equivalency for each course.
